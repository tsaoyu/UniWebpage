About me

My name is Yu Cao (Tony), PhD student in Fluid Structure Interactions research group.

My research topic is the application of renewable and hybrid energy system on marine vehicles. In this research, I will investigate the possibility of using energy scavenging system to improve the efficiency of the marine vehicles.

My other research interests are: autonomous system, control theory and system engineering.
